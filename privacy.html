Last updated: December 8, 2024

1. Information We Collect

We believe in privacy first. We only collect the minimal information necessary to provide our service:

  • Usage Data: We monitor server performance, which may include IP addresses and connection logs, purely for technical troubleshooting and security purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely to:

  • Process your subscription and activate your service.
  • Send important account updates, such as renewal reminders.
  • Improve the performance and stability of our servers.

3. Data Protection

We implement industry-standard security measures, including SSL encryption, to protect your personal information during transmission and storage. We do not sell your data to third parties.

4. Cookies

Our website uses essential cookies to ensure functionality (e.g., keeping you logged in). We do not use tracking cookies for advertising purposes.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to your personal data or request its deletion. If you wish to exercise these rights, please contact our support team.

6. Contact Us

For any privacy-related concerns, please reach out to us.
